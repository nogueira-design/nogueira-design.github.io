EBAC

2022 - 2024

During my projects with Itaú/EBAC and EBAC's final course project, I gained practical, hands-on experience in the complete UX/UI design process. These experiences have made me a more empathetic, thorough, and user-focused designer.

The first project, a UX/UI case study for Itaú, taught me to deconstruct user flows and identify pain points, such as app instability and difficulty viewing investments. By conducting research and creating a user journey, I learned to approach design with a focus on empathy and a deep understanding of the user. This experience has since shaped my design work, with a strong focus on creating clean, intuitive, and reliable interfaces that address real-world user challenges.

The second project, a final course project, involved redesigning an outdated website for a public competitive exam company. This project allowed me to apply the full Double Diamond design process, from initial discovery and research to creating a final, accessible prototype. Using methods like benchmarking, user surveys, and usability tests, I learned to translate user pain points into effective design solutions.

Combined, these experiences have significantly enhanced my ability to create robust, intuitive, and aesthetically pleasing interfaces. I now approach my design work with a stronger focus on preventing errors and creating user-friendly systems that are reliable and truly address user needs.
