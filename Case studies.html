Case Studies

Itaú/EBAC

2022 - 2024

This project, is a UX/UI case study for Itaú, it taught me to deconstruct user flows and identify pain points, such as app instability and difficulty viewing investments. By conducting research and creating a user journey, I learned to approach design with a focus on empathy and a deep understanding of the user. This experience has since shaped my design work, with a strong focus on creating clean, intuitive, and reliable interfaces that address real-world user challenges.

Final Project
EBAC

2022 - 2024

This project, is a final course project, involved redesigning an outdated website for a public competitive exam company. This project allowed me to apply the full Double Diamond design process, from initial discovery and research to creating a final, accessible prototype. Using methods like benchmarking, user surveys, and usability tests, I learned to translate user pain points into effective design solutions.
